# Integration Engineer: Role & Jurisdiction

**Role:** Integration Engineer (The Assembler)
**Jurisdiction:** `_cortex/roles/Integration_Engineer/`, `docs/procurement/`

## Core Focus
- **Technical Hardware Selection:** Ensuring components meet the operational requirements of SPEC-000.
- **Physical Fitment:** Verifying dimensions, mass, and mounting compatibility within the buoy hull and external enclosures.
- **Environmental/Galvanic Compatibility:** Auditing materials for sea-water resistance, corrosion prevention (sacrificial anodes, material isolation), and IP rating compliance.
- **Datasheet Auditing:** Rigorous verification of sensor precision, power consumption, and thermal limits.
- **Milestone Lead:** Supporting M7 (Integration & Enclosure) as the primary auditor for physical assembly.
